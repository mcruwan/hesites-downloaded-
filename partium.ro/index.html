Bachelor’s Degree
Build your expertise
A bachelor’s degree from the PCU will not only give you a broad and competitive range of knowledge in one or more fields, but it will also help you build professional contacts that can help you realize your career. Our high-standard courses are delivered by renowned national and international professors with great prowess, they contain domestic and foreign study trips, with possibilities of scholarships and bursary opportunities to support your professional development.
Discover our BA courses
Master’s Degree
Be the master of your craft
Besides teaching, our professors provide career advice and help students make professional contacts. A Master’s degree is also an advantage in the job market, opening up career opportunities that require higher qualifications. Our project-based training strategy helps our students every day so they can get their first taste of their profession before they graduate.
Find out more about our MA courses
Teacher training
Specialized teacher training
The Department for Teacher Training of the Partium Christian University in Oradea provides basic and in-depth teacher training for those who wish to enroll in the Level I and II pedagogical modules during their studies, moreover postgraduate training for those who decide to enter the teaching profession after completing their university studies. It is a complementary module to any major.
Go to the Pedagogy module
Partium Language Center
Foreign language courses and exams
The Partium Language Center organizes language courses and specialized language courses for all age groups, at all levels (beginner, intermediate, advanced) — in English, German, French, Italian, Hungarian, and Romanian. It also provides students and residents of the region with the preparation and examination for the Partium, Cambridge, and ECL language examinations (English, German, French); additionally translation and interpretation services too.
Go to the Language Center page
 
Science
Research and innovation
Scientific and artistic research is a permanent feature of the PCU’s activities, involving both our teachers and students. Over the years, several research groups have been set up, either within departments or through interdepartmental collaboration, to regularly carry out research projects, conferences, and other specialized programs.

International life
Erasmus+ and international opportunities across borders
The Erasmus+ and Makovecz programs are designed to provide students and academic staff with the opportunity to acquire professional experience abroad, support their study and research activities, and participate in specialized projects and networks. Through these partnerships, participants can acquire competitive knowledge on a European level, learn about other cultures, and practice their foreign language skills.
