48 Training programs

Dai Nam University (DNU) is a multidisciplinary institution comprising 50 affiliated units, strategically developed around three core pillars: Application - Entrepreneurship & Innovation - International Integration. The university offers 48 diverse academic programs (including 42 undergraduate programs, 5 master’s programs, and 1 doctoral program) with a streamlined, flexible learning pathway. Its modern curriculum shortens the study period by 0.5 to 1 year, enabling students to graduate early, secure immediate employment, and confidently seize career opportunities in the era of global integration.

Undergraduate Programs

With 42 modern, practical, and flexible undergraduate programs, Dai Nam University provides students with an advanced learning environment closely tied to professional practices and the evolving trends of societal development. Notably, the training duration is shortened by 0.5 to 1 year, enabling students to graduate earlier, quickly adapt to the job market, and seize career opportunities in an era of integration. “Graduate Fast – Work Immediately” – Dai Nam University stands by your side on the journey to conquer your future!

Master's Programs

Dai Nam University offers 5 high-quality Master’s programs, meticulously designed with depth and practical relevance, enabling learners not only to enhance their expertise but also to elevate their thinking, expand opportunities, and establish their standing in their respective fields. With a faculty of leading experts, training programs aligned with the latest trends, and a modern learning environment, learners will be equipped with a solid knowledge foundation, exceptional leadership skills, and strategic thinking skills to break through in their careers.

Doctoral Programs

International Joint Programs

The International Partnership Training Program at Dai Nam University opens up opportunities for learning and experiencing a world-class educational environment. Through close collaboration with international universities, educational organizations, and businesses, students and learners not only gain access to cutting-edge knowledge but also develop a global mindset and enhance their competitive edge worldwide.

Pathways - 2nd Degree - Online

The Pathways - 2nd Degree - Online training program at Dai Nam University offer outstanding advantages, enabling learners to study flexibly and effectively. With high-quality training programs, learners will be equipped with solid professional knowledge and practical skills, while earning a nationally recognized regular degree. Notably, the programs allow learning anytime, anywhere, helping you flexibly manage your study schedule without disrupting work or personal life.

Why choose Dai Nam University

A flexible academic pathway offering optimal choices for every learning journey:

• Undergraduate Programs: Integrating theory with practice, shortening study duration, connecting with businesses — preparing students for immediate employment.

• Master’s Programs: Advancing in-depth knowledge, staying updated with trends — enhancing strategic thinking.

• Doctoral Programs: Focusing on research and generating breakthrough value in the field of economics.

• International Joint Training: Opportunities for studying abroad and internships at leading universities worldwide.

• University Transfer, Second Degrees, and E-Distance Learning: Flexible, standardized, and nationally recognized.

At Dai Nam University, every student can choose the right major, learn the right way, and grow in the right direction!

With over 100 international partners and more than 1,000 domestic and global corporate partners, Dai Nam University is not only a place of learning, but also a strong foundation for students to broaden their horizons, enhance their competitiveness, and successfully integrate into the global workforce.

Student life

At Dai Nam University, learning is not only the acquisition of knowledge but also a transformative journey to unlock potential and foster holistic development in intellect, skills, and core values. With hundreds of student-led activities, clubs, and community service programs, Dai Nam is a nurturing ground where young people cultivate resilience, emotional depth, and a strong desire to contribute to society.
