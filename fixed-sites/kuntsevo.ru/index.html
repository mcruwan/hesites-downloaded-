    Trade and Industrial Group “KUNTSEVO”

    Trade and Industrial Group “KUNTSEVO” founded in 1992. Currently, it is an agro-industrial complex, which includes:

    • land for growing potatoes, oats, wheat, barley.
    • processing enterprises in the Vologda, Ryazan and Belgorod regions.
