Determined to go forward

Determined to go forward. We'll help you on your way
00:27

Today, everything seems simple. Everythings seems easy. A career in three months, workation straight from the hammock, and work tasks accompanied by nothing but good vibes. It’s easy to be captivated by this vision, and even easier to be disappointed. However, those who have accomplished much in life understand that true success is attained by those who do not opt for the easiest paths but are willing to work and fight fiercely. Not against others, but against themselves.

ISM is designed specifically for such determined intellect adventurers. It’s a realm of science for those who understand that achieving results may require rolling up their sleeves and, at times, diving headfirst into challenges. No, we won’t offer a pre-prepared escape route each time, but a strong and supportive community – always. This will inspire the pursuit of knowledge and openness to innovation. Most importantly, it encourages not giving up after the first failure, and even more so after the tenth.

For those aspiring to professional heights – those aiming for more – ISM promises to be both a motivating challenge and a strong support. Be ready to move forward.

About ISM

ISM – a community uniting everyone who wants to be one step ahead

It is an international university in Vilnius that brings together teachers and students from all over the world. Here everyone receives individual attention, modern teaching methods are applied and extremely close relations with business are maintained. With us, students start their careers from the very first day, get to know business representatives and build their own personal business network.
Read more
International Studies Office

Double degree and exchange opportunities

Students often call ISM University a foreign land within Lithuania, because the international community of students and lecturers is extremely friendly here. Almost all subjects are taught in English. Students have the opportunity to earn double degrees – ISM and BI Norwegian Business SchoolBI Norwegian Business School), KEDGE verslo mokyklos (KEDGE Business School(France) or the Illinois Institute of Technology (USA). ISM offers exchange opportunities with more than 100 foreign universities.

Career center

Your career from the first day at university

The goal of the ISM Career Center is to help each student own his career. To achieve it, we provide personal attention to each student during career consultations. As we partner with various organizations and the best Lithuanian and international companies, where we organize meetings with business representatives. During individual consultations, we help you choose an organization based on your aspirations, needs and strengths.

Research

International research at ISM University

ISM bases its scientific activities on the creation and application of new knowledge and ideas in economy, politics and business. International research is directed at the increasing challenges our society, economy and business are facing and seeks to influence and meet the needs of today’s society.
